The Top Beer Bars in Toronto

Are you a brew lover? Well If so, you need to visit Toronto. The breweries are unmatched and not only places to try but, also places to meet new friends & expand your brew buds.

Stout Irish Pub

Beer and Trivia pub located in Cabbagetown,Toronto.

Otto's Bierhalle

If you want good lighting, and hanging vines with the heartiness of solid german food and beer.

BeerBistro

A classy Bistro where it would be acceptable to bring your parents or boss.

Tallboys-Craft Beer House

Holds the title for carrying the most cans in the city, with a strong focus on Ontario craft.

Tequila Bookworm

Has a vibe of a college bar with graffiti wall art, but the beer and wine list speak for itself.

Bar Volo

Beer drinkers across Toronto gasp when you bring this bar up.

Bar Hop Bar

The ideal spot to meet friends after work or before the profesional competitions.
